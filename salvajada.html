Salvajada recipe

The salchipapa salvajada is a delicious and hearty culinary creation originating in Colombia that has become a favorite among street food lovers. This irresistible combination fuses the classic elements of the simple salchipapa and various types of meats and sausages, creating a unique and satisfying gastronomic experience.

INGREDIENTS

  • c/n Precooked potato
  • 1 lb mozzarella or heavy cream cheese
  • 24 units chorizo
  • 24 units chicken wings
  • 24 units traditional sausage
  • 2 lb ground beef
  • 1 breast
  • 1 lettuce
  • c/n Sauces of choice
  • Beer or wine
  • Onion- Garlic
  • Chili powder

STEPS

  1. We put in half a liter of water to cook a breast with garlic, onion and thyme, and then crumble it.
  2. We start marinating the meat with spices to taste, I will use pepper, seasoning, salt, let stand 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. Finely chop garlic and onion preferably (I will use purple) placed to fry in a skillet over medium heat for two minutes with a pinch of salt.
  4. In our mixture with onion add the meat previously seasoned with a splash of wine or beer and let simmer and stir constantly until cooked.
  5. The wings we put them to cook in a skillet over medium heat, optional prepare a sauce, I leave the recipe on my profile.
  6. In a deep frying pan or cauldron we put to heat oil over medium-low heat.
  7. Fry the potatoes and place them in a bowl or deep bowl and absorbent paper on the bottom, when they are all cooked, add salt to taste and optional a pinch of chili powder.
  8. Fry the sausages, the cut is optional.
  9. Preferably use cheese block to grate.
  10. Emplatamos with a layer of potato, meat, lettuce, breast, sausage, cheese and wings, add sauces to taste.
