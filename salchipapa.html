Salchipapa recipe

Salchipapas are one of the most delicious dishes in Latin America. Today we show you step by step how to prepare a homemade Colombian salchipapa, tasty, economical and very easy to make. This recipe is ideal for those days when you are craving for fast food and you don't want to leave home, come and try it!

INGREDIENTS

  • 200 grs Cheese (Any type of grated cheese to taste)
  • 15 sausages
  • 1 kg Potatoes (Potatoes for frying)
  • Chongo potato
  • 1 Lettuce
  • 1 Lemon juice
  • 1 can of baby corn
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt (To taste)
  • Mayonnaise (Amount to taste)
  • Pink Sauce (Amount to taste)
  • Pineapple sauce (Amount to taste)

STEPS

  1. To start preparing this delicious salchipapas recipe, wash the potatoes very well, peel them and cut them into strips.
  2. Put the cut potatoes in a bowl with water, lemon juice and salt to taste. Let them stand for 30 minutes.
  3. After this time, drain and dry the potatoes very well with a paper towel or absorbent cloth.
  4. Heat the oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. When the oil is very hot, fry the potatoes for 5 to 8 minutes or until they begin to brown. Remove and drain on a paper towel.
  6. Now it's time for the sausages. Cut all the sausages into pieces of any size you like. This time, we have opted for a cross cut at the ends to give them a fun look.
  7. Toss the sausages in the same oil in which you fried the potatoes. Once they have colored, remove and drain.
  8. Place the fries on a large platter or plate. Then place the sausages on top.
  9. Cut the lettuce leaves into strips.
  10. Finally add the lettuce strips, the chongo potato, the pineapple sauce, the mayonnaise and the pink sauce.
  11. Add the grated cheese, again the salsa and the baby corn and that's it, enjoy!
